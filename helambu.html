Experience Nepal's Breathtaking Treks

Welcome to NepalTreks, your guide to the most spectacular hiking destinations in Nepal. From the sacred lakes of Panch Pokhari to the majestic peaks of Langtang, we'll help you plan your perfect Himalayan adventure.

Featured Destination: Ama Yangri

Experience the stunning sunrise views from Ama Yangri peak (3,771m), a sacred site with panoramic Himalayan views just a short trek from Kathmandu. Perfect for beginners and spiritual seekers alike.

Popular Himalayan Treks

Ama Yangri Trek

A short but rewarding trek offering spectacular views of Langtang, Ganesh Himal, and even Everest on clear days. The sacred summit features prayer flags and a small shrine.

Duration: 2-3 days

Difficulty: Moderate

Trek Details

Panch Pokhari Trek

Journey to the sacred five lakes at 4,100m, a pilgrimage site with breathtaking views. This off-the-beaten-path trek takes you through beautiful rhododendron forests and traditional Tamang villages.

Duration: 10-12 days

Difficulty: Challenging

Trek Details

Langtang Valley Trek

Explore the "Valley of Glaciers" with its stunning mountain scenery, diverse wildlife, and rich Tamang culture. One of Nepal's most beautiful yet less crowded trekking regions.

Duration: 7-10 days

Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
